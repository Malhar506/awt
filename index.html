The Slazanger was Founded in 1881 by Ralph and Albert Slazenger.

  • Our mainly products are the balls for the outdoor sports like Lawn Tennis and Golf.
  • Generally the Products:-
    Tennis :- Rackets, balls, shoes
    Golf :- Clubs, balls
    Swimming :- Swimsuits
  • Clothing (general) :- T-shirts, polo shirts, jackets, hoodies, fleece jackets, sweaters, shorts
    Accessories (general):- Bags, watches, sunglasses

We have the offical parternship with the WIMBELDON for supplying Tennis Balls from almost more than the 120+ years.

Why Choose Slazenger

We have the longest parternship with WIMBELDON in the history of Lawn Tennis with more than 120+ years and we have been manufacturing the products like Tennis balls and Rackets and sports clothes from more 50+ years.
